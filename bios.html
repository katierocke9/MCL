For the next few years, I traveled throughout Europe, Southeast Asia, and Africa teaching and being involved in a few global educational programs:
  • Taught English at a High School in Torino, Italy
  • Taught English online to children in China
  • Developed professional development training programs in Penang, Malaysia
  • Team Leader for the LRTT Professional Develop Training Program in Tanzania, Africa
For the last few years I’ve done private and group tutoring sessions for children in K-12 grades, and homeschooling a 4th grader and a High School student.

My future goal is to design and build a learning community that lives by its philosophy through its mentors, coaches, parents, and children. I want to re-establish a learning environment that focuses on individual needs, interests, and learning levels while building a love for learning.

Emotional Awareness and Conscious will be an important part of the learning process.

  • Parent/ art volunteer for the Academy of Arts, Careers, and Technology preschool program
  • Child and Family Research Center Lead Preschool Teacher UNR, Reno NV
  • Little Learners II, Reno NV Preschool Director, and Kinder Program Teacher
  • Sunrise Preschool, Lead Pre-K Master Teacher
  • Municipal Childcare Center, Relief Child Minder
  • Municipal Childcare preschool teacher floaters/substitute
My passion always has been to educate the whole child in a developmentally appropriate and play-based manner in a living classroom. Learning is as much in your body as it is in your head. “Hands-on” experiences or manipulatives during the learning process greatly increase learning efficiency. Touch is a strong anchor in behavior and learning. Children’s movement cannot be restricted but should always be included in as many ways as possible. Children need to be able to express and label their emotions to develop a strong emotional vocabulary. The classroom environment should be the “third teacher” that supports learning and routines with manipulatives and access to self-correcting and open-ended materials.
Furthermore, my goal is to instill the love of learning and exploration in each child to develop intrinsic motivation. To me, the goal in education begins by meeting each child where they are and supporting the family so each child can develop and grow to their full potential in a developmentally appropriate manner.
