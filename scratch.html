Marly - Head Learning Mentor/Director


I’ve been an educator for 10 years in diverse settings; both globally and locally. I have a Master's Degree in Secondary Education from the University of Nevada, Reno, and taught at the High School level for 3 years. For the next few years, I traveled throughout Europe, Southeast Asia, and Africa teaching and being involved in a few global educational programs:
  • Taught English at a High School in Torino, Italy
  • Taught English online to children in China
  • Developed professional development training programs in Penang, Malaysia
  • Team Leader for the LRTT Professional Develop Training Program in Tanzania, Africa
For the last few years I’ve done private and group tutoring sessions for children in K-12 grades, and homeschooling a 4th grader and a High School student.

My future goal is to design and build a learning community that lives by its philosophy through its mentors, coaches, parents, and children. I want to re-establish a learning environment that focuses on individual needs, interests, and learning levels while building a love for learning.

Emotional Awareness and Conscious will be an important part of the learning process.
